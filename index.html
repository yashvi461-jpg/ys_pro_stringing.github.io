Process

Simple 4-Step Process

From first contact to pickup, I make restringing easy and convenient.

1
Place Your Order

Contact me via text, WhatsApp, or Instagram DM. Tell me about your racket, string preferences, and desired tension.

2
Drop Off

Bring your racket to my home-based location in San Carlos. Convenient contactless drop-off at the door.

3
I String It

Your racket is professionally strung on my Gamma XLT electric machine with precision and care.

4
Pickup Ready

Within 48 hours, you'll be notified. Pick up your freshly strung racket via contactless pickup.
